What is the Rekt League?

League of Legends is a game that has created and destroyed friendships, stolen countless hours from dedicated players, and entertains so many people on a daily basis.

The best parts about league aren't actually playing the game anymore, but the community behind it banding together to create an amazing esport.

We wanted to create our own little community that exists outside of professional play.

This league is for the bronzes to diamonds that have not had the chance to taste a real structured format for this game. A lot of league players will squad up with friends but never get that true LCS experience of preparing for your opponents.

With this in mind, we highly encourage try-harding and decimating your opponents, but more importantly have fun with this! Most importantly make sick plays so we can clip them.

When you join this league you will start playing games with a lot of strangers, after you have been in it you will be playing a game you love with new friends.
