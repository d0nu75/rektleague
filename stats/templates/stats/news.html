Welcome to The Rekt League! The Rekt League is a competitve League of Legends tournaments series and community for the people. Our goal is to create a fun and competitve place for all ranks to play organized League of Legends while also trying to provide a high production value stream for the games. We think that playing League FEELS a lot different when they're treated more like LCS than flex queue. We want to cater to the players that want to play with their friends, but also want a new and unique challenge.

If you're looking to join The Rekt League explore the site, follow us, and email us at therektleague.com with your intent to join the single greatest tournament series to ever bless Twitch.
